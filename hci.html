Carl-Mart

In the class Human Computer Interaction, I worked with three students, Wesley Yang, Khizar Qureshi, and Sho Tanaka in order to create an application that would solve a problem here at Carleton College. We decided to focus on waster as well as providing opportunities for all individuals to get items necessary for college students. To do so we decided to create an application in which students can buy, sell, or trade items. This provides opportunities for students to get lightly used items that would normally be thrown away, from other students at cheap, affordable pricing.
